Welcome to BDER.

BDER is a simplistic user-interface application for the GSAL Biology Department. It is usable and stylish, combining modern UI elements and straightforward controls to provide the sleekest of performances with the daily life of the Biology Technicians and the Biology Staff.

Support or Contact

Having trouble with BDER? Please submit an issue to this page and I'll respond as soon as possible.

How do I file an issue?

As of Version 3.4.0.3, simply click the "Feedback" button and you're good to go.
